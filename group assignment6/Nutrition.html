Nutrition Plans

Day 1

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast, Greek yogurt with berries.

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette.

Snack: Apple slices with almond butter.

Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli.

Day 2

Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with sliced bananas, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.

Lunch: Lentil soup with a side of whole-grain crackers.

Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.

Dinner: Grilled tofu with brown rice and sautéed spinach.

Day 3

Breakfast: Whole-grain pancakes with fresh strawberries and a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, and feta cheese.

Snack: Mixed nuts and dried fruits.

Dinner: Lean beef stir-fry with mixed vegetables and brown rice.

Day 4

Breakfast: Avocado toast on whole-grain bread with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.

Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side of carrot sticks.

Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with couscous and roasted asparagus.

Day 5

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder.

Lunch: Spinach and quinoa-stuffed bell peppers.

Snack: Edamame with sea salt.

Dinner: Baked chicken breast with sweet potato wedges and steamed green beans.
